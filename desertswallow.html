Desert Swallow

Background

First half of the 2 semester Capstone Design entailed designing a full scale Formula 1 Air Race plane for the Reno Air Race

Main Requirements:
  • FAR 21 Experimental Certification
  • Flight VFR Compliant
  • 10,000 MSL Service Ceiling
  • Cockpit Suitable for 1 pilot (Max: 6'1" & 200lbs)
  • Capable of 300nm range plus VFR reserves
